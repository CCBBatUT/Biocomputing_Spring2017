Welcome!

This is the official website for the Spring 2017 Peer-led Biocomputing Group at UT Austin, offered through the Center for Computational Biology and Bioinformatics. The goal of this class is to take away any fear of programming that you may have so that computing can become an advantage rather than a barrier to your research.

The class will meet on Fridays from 1-2 PM in PAT 143. In addition, feel free to join us for Open Coding Hour at Wednesdays 4-5 PM in the CCBB conference room (GDC 7.514) for any extra help or feedback.

Please use the links to the left for contact information and some useful biocomputing resources. Please also feel free to participate in the Biocomputing Google Groups Forum with any questions that you have as you begin your biocomputing journey!

Installation and Setup Instructions

We will be connecting to the CCBB's Biocomputing Pod, which requires you to set up both a TACC account and an RCTF account. Instructions are located here. It can take a few days for the RCTF account creation to go through.

It's a good idea to also install a text editor. If you are using a Mac, some excellent options are either Text Wrangler, Sublime Text, or Atom. Linux users have a built-in text editor called gedit. Windows users can check out Notepad++.

If you need any help, come to Open Coding Hour or email Katie at klyons@gmail.com
